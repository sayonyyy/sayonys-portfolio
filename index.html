My Projects

FitTech

FitTech is a fitness-focused web app where users enter their height, weight, and age to get personalized diet plans, workouts, and sports-based routines. It provides video guidance, and sport-specific recommendations. Future updates will include a daily progress tracking, and an integrated store, allowing users to instantly purchase diet food, supplements, and sports accessories directly from the platform — so they never have to pause their fitness journey due to lack of resources. FitTech aims to offer a one-stop solution for fitness, diet, and training — even enabling gym access and health-based personalization using prescriptions.

Coffee Ecom

A visually aesthetic eCommerce platform tailored for coffee lovers. It offers an elegant UI/UX, wide coffee variety, and a smooth online shopping experience. Designed to stand out from typical eCom apps with a richer look and premium feel.
